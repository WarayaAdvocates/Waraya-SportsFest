📋 General Information

  • The Sportsfest is themed after Avatar: The Last Airbender, featuring four (4) competing teams, each representing one of the four nations.
  • Each team may produce and wear its own team shirt with its assigned team color. All members are encouraged to wear designated team colors.
  • All emeritus and regular members shall be divided into four (4) groups based on total registrants.
  • The Technical Working Group (TWG) shall determine team composition and assign participants.
  • The TWG shall not be part of the competing teams and serves solely as the organizing body.
  • Each team shall designate a Team Leader and identify official players for each game category.

🏐 Volleyball Guidelines (Mixed Category)

  • Minimum of ten (10) members per team (any gender).
  • Six (6) main players on court; four (4) substitutes.
  • Round Robin Format (4 teams) → 6 matches total.
  • Top 2 teams → Championship Match; 3rd & 4th → Battle for 3rd Place.
  • Scoring: Best-of-3 sets, each set to 16 points (must win by 2).
  • Two (2) timeouts per set allowed.
  • Players must wear proper sports attire; knee pads highly recommended.
  • Based on FIVB Rule Book.

📱 Mobile Legends Guidelines

  • Each team: Five (5) main players + One (1) substitute.
  • No replacement allowed once registered.
  • Round Robin Format (4 teams).
  • Top 2 → Championship Match; 3rd & 4th → Battle for 3rd.
  • Two (2) team pauses + One (1) technical pause per match.
  • 15-minute grace period; failure to report results in forfeit.
  • Players must ensure stable internet connection.

🔤 Scrabble Guidelines

  • Each team designates one (1) official representative.
  • Round Robin Format; highest match points declared Champion.
  • Standard 15×15 board with 100 tiles (including two blank tiles).
  • Two (2) minutes per turn; failure to play results in forfeiture.
  • All words must be valid English words (no proper nouns, abbreviations).
  • Fifty (50)-point bonus for using all seven (7) tiles in one turn.
  • No dictionaries, mobile phones, or electronic devices allowed.
  • Scoring: Win = 3 pts, Draw = 1 pt, Loss = 0 pts
  • Tie-breakers: Highest cumulative score → Head-to-head → Tie-breaker match

🏓 Pickleball Guidelines

  • Three categories: Men's Doubles, Women's Doubles, Mixed Doubles
  • Open to all players regardless of age and skill level.
  • A player may participate in only one (1) category.
  • Match schedules posted via Reclub application.
  • Ten (10)-minute grace period; failure to appear results in default.
  • Governed by official Pickleball rules.

♟️ Chess Tournament Guidelines (Standard Category)

  • Four (4) teams, each with one (1) official representative.
  • Single Round Robin System (6 matches total).
  • Follows official FIDE Standard Chess Rules.
  • Time Control: 10-15 minutes per player.
  • Scoring: Win = 1 pt, Draw = 0.5 pt, Loss = 0 pt
  • Touch-move rule applies.
  • Tie-breakers: Head-to-head → Number of wins → Sonneborn-Berger → Playoff

The Organizing Committee reserves the right to amend, modify, or revise these guidelines as may be necessary to ensure the smooth, orderly, and fair conduct of the tournament.
